Conflict

---Week 8

In most conflicts, one feels shame, and fear. InBrene Brown's TED talk, she talks about how shame is correlated with suicide, depression, etc., but guilt inversely correlated.

As well, when talking about shame, it requires secrecy, silence, and judgement to grow. In terms of conflict, when I've looked back, it necessitates some of the same requiements. What's useful, if possible, is to talk about the source of the conflict, supress judgement (as hard as it may be), and try to be an open book.
