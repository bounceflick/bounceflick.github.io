An issue facing the tech industry: Is there a bubble?

Possibly the issues of most relevance to me is whether there's a tech bubble - is it primed for a crash as we've seen earlier this century? This has immediate consequences in terms of employability and direct income.

While if there is a crash, my employment prospects will suffer, I'm comfortable in the knowledge I'm building a long term skill set. This should stand the test of time, whether boom or bust.
