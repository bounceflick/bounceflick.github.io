Pairing:

What was it like to pair with others to solve challenges?

It's great fun! Everyone has something to contribute!

Was something particularly fun or rewarding?

It's great when someone has a different approach or style of looking at things.

Was something frustrating and difficult for you?

Sometimes reminding yourself to stay with one role (driver or navigator, not both), can prove challenging

Feedback:

How did you feel when you read your feedback?

It's key to keep in the growth mindset and use feedback as an opportunity to learn.

Was the feedback you received helpful to your learning?

Yes, very helpful!

Based on the feedback you've received, what are you going to do to improve next time you pair?

Set the objective and time limit up front, as well as a timer to switch roles.

How was it to write feedback?

Giving great feedback is hard!

What was most difficult for you when writing feedback for your pair?

Giving useful feedback when I felt the other person really had nothing to improve.

Overall, what do you think of using pairing and feedback to guide your learning?

It's been great!
