Stereotype Threat

---Week 6

I think we've all experienced stereotype threat - sometimes self-inflicted pain, where you judge yourself before other judge you.

What I'd like to do when I experience it or others experience it is first recognize it - both internally and externally. I think expressing discomfort or asking if someone else is uncomfortable is the first step. I'd love to keep discussion about data, vs. feelings, but understand this is very difficult.
